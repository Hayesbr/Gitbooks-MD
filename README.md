# Welcome to Diversifi

Diversifi is a parcel intelligence platform built for third-party logistics providers. It helps you win more business with data-driven rate bids, automate your carrier billing reconciliation, and give your customers accurate, transparent bills — without the manual spreadsheet work.

## What You Can Do in Diversifi

**BidBoost** — Build carrier rate bids for prospects in minutes. Upload a shipment file or enter package dimensions, select your carriers, apply your markup, and export a polished rate card to send directly to your prospect.

**Billing** — Automate your carrier invoice reconciliation. Ingest invoices from your carriers, apply your billing rules, and generate accurate customer bills without manual spreadsheet work.

**Integrations** — Connect your WMS and carrier accounts so data flows automatically into the platform. Diversifi supports Extensiv, ShipHero, ShipStation, and more.

## Where to Start

If you are new to Diversifi, start here:

1. Set up your account and invite your team
2. Connect your WMS integration
3. Load your carrier cost rates
4. Run your first bid
5. Set up your first billing cycle

The [Getting Started](getting-started.md) guide walks through each of these steps in order.
