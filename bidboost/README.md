# BidBoost Overview

BidBoost is Diversifi's carrier rate bidding tool. It helps 3PL sales reps build accurate, professional rate proposals for prospects in minutes — without spreadsheets, manual calculations, or back-and-forth.

## What BidBoost Does

When a brand is evaluating a 3PL, one of the first things they want to know is: what will my shipping cost? BidBoost answers that question by taking your negotiated carrier rates, applying your markup, and producing a polished rate card the prospect can actually use to make a decision.

The result is a deliverable you can send directly to the prospect — a net rate card, a blended rate comparison, or a full shipment file analysis showing exactly what their shipping would cost if they moved to your 3PL.

## The Three Bid Types

BidBoost supports three types of bids depending on what data you have from the prospect.

**PLD — Shipment File Bid**
The most accurate bid type. The prospect shares their historical shipment data — a file of actual shipments with weights, dimensions, and destination ZIP codes, called Package Level Detail (PLD) or a shipment file. Diversifi rates every shipment against your carrier costs and markup to show exactly what each package would have cost if it had been shipped by the 3PL. **Use this when** the prospect can share shipment history; it's the most compelling deliverable because it uses their real data.

**SKU Bid**
The prospect shares their product catalog — SKU dimensions and weights — instead of historical shipments. Diversifi rates each SKU across your carriers and zones. **Use this when** the prospect knows their products but doesn't have shipment history readily available.

**No Data Bid**
A market rate comparison using aggregated industry data. No prospect data required. Diversifi generates a rate card based on the most common weight and zone profile in your market. **Use this when** the prospect can't or won't share data but you still want to show them a rate comparison — good for early-stage conversations.

## How a Bid Flows

Every bid follows the same basic flow regardless of type:

1. **Select or add a prospect** — who is this bid for
2. **Choose your bid type** — PLD, SKU, or No Data
3. **Select carriers and services** — which of your carriers you want to include
4. **Apply your markup** — set your margin per carrier or service
5. **Review and export** — download the rate card to send to the prospect

The entire process takes under 10 minutes once your cost rates are loaded.

## What You Get Out

BidBoost produces three export types depending on what the prospect needs:

- **Net Rate Export** — a carrier rate card showing the proposed rate for every weight and zone combination. It shows your markup applied to your cost rates but never reveals your actual costs.
- **Blended Rate Export** — a comparison of your cheapest rate per weight and zone across all selected carriers. Useful when you want to show the prospect the best rate available regardless of carrier.
- **Shipment File Export** — a full analysis of the prospect's actual shipments, showing what each package would have cost, which carrier won, and how much they would save compared to their current rates.

## Before You Run Your First Bid

Make sure you have completed the following in Diversifi:

- Cost rates loaded for all carriers you want to include in bids
- Carrier configurations set up for each carrier
- Warehouses added with the correct injection points for regional carriers
- Rate sets assigned to the relevant warehouses

If you haven't done this yet, start with the [Cost Rates](cost-rates.md) article.
