# Support

We're here to help you get the most out of Diversifi.

## Search First

The fastest way to get an answer is the search bar at the top of any Knowledge Center page — our AI will find the relevant answer from this documentation instantly. Most questions about setup, bidding, billing, and integrations are answered in the articles here.

## Contact Support

If you can't find what you need, reach out to us:

- **Email:** [support@diversifi.ai](mailto:support@diversifi.ai)
- **Your account manager:** Your dedicated Diversifi account manager is your direct line for setup help, configuration questions, and anything specific to your account.

## When to Contact Your Account Manager

Some tasks are handled with your account manager directly:

- Connecting or configuring your QuickBooks integration
- Setting up Extensiv's billing module for full quoted-cost reconciliation
- Adding a carrier that isn't currently supported for automatic invoice ingest
- Questions about API access and documentation
- Resolving a significant billing dispute before the next billing period
- Confirming how your specific WMS records data (for example, multi-shipment cost behavior)

## Helpful Tips Before You Reach Out

To help us resolve your question quickly:

- For a bid or rate question, note the prospect/bid name and use the **Validation View** to see exactly how a rate was calculated — many pricing questions can be answered there. See [Exports](bidboost/exports.md).
- For a billing question, note the customer, billing period, and bill section (Parcel, Order, Adjustments, or Other Fees).
- For an integration or sync issue, check the integration status panel for the specific error message before contacting support.
